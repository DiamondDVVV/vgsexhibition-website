RAJYA SABHA

RAJYA SABHA: The upper house of India's Parliament. Unlike the Lok Sabha, members of the Rajya Sabha are not directly elected but are appointed or elected indirectly by the states.

FUNCTIONS

The Rajya Sabha reviews and revises the bills passed by the Lok Sabha. It ensures that laws are thoroughly analyzed before becoming enforceable.
