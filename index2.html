MIGRATION

MIGRATION: The movement of a person or people from one country, locality, or place of residence to settle in another. It's a process that impacts many aspects of life.

POPULATION

POPULATION: The entire number of people living in a particular area or region. Population changes are key indicators of economic and social health.

DEATH RATE

DEATH RATE: A measure of the number of deaths in a particular area during a given period, usually expressed per 1,000 people per year.
